I belong to the generation of entrepreneurs who believe in Africa. I am a UX Designer, Web Developer, Digital Marketer, Graphic Design and a Business and Communication professional.

I see myself as a Consultant in Internet based services as well as Project Management in the 3 years. In 10 years, I see myself mentor young people in my area of expertise and being an international business leader with strong acumen and great influence. Yes I can!
